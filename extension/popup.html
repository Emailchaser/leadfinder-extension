Lead Finder

Open Sales Navigator lead search
How it works
  1. Open LinkedIn Sales Navigator and run a search.
  2. Click "Export with Emailchaser" on the page (the button we add).
  3. Your Emailchaser account will open to process that search context.

This extension saves the current Sales Navigator search context locally and then opens the Emailchaser web app where your account performs enrichment and export. The extension itself does not find emails or generate CSV files.

Requirements & permissions

Requires an Emailchaser account and access to LinkedIn Sales Navigator.

Uses storage to hold the chosen search context and webRequest/host permissions to detect the Sales Navigator request you export.
